Claire Boucher was born in Vancouver in 1988. Before beginning her music career, she attended McGill University, studying neuroscience and psychology, amongst other things. She has been releasing music under the name Grimes since 2007. Recently, Claire has changed her name to a shorter “C.”, which is the symbol for the speed of light.

There isn’t one particular style to describe Grimes music. Her albums include a mix of electronic pop, R&B, and synth-pop. The use of layering vocal tracks gives her music a unique echo sound. Since her start in 2007, she has released five studio albums, eleven singles, and fifteen music videos.

Grimes has been in a relationship with Elon Musk since 2018. They just recently had their first child in May of 2020.

Doubling as a visual artist, Grimes creates all of her album art by herself. She credits her inspiration to anime and comic books.
